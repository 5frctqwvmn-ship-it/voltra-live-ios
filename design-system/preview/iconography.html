Iconography & Brand

A wordmark, an app mark, and a small set of SF Symbols. Icons never carry meaning alone — every glyph in this app has a label next to it.

App mark

VOLTRA Live
3 nested teal triangles · #00d4aa on #0a0e0c
Source: AppIcon.appiconset/icon-1024.png

The mark is three nested teal triangles, pointing up, on the canvas color. The triangles step from #005a47 (outer, dim) through #00a085 (mid) to #00d4aa (inner, accent) — a literal "stepping up to the lift" metaphor.

The wordmark sets VOLTRA in caps + 700 weight, with Live in matching weight, no italic, no separator. They share a baseline.

For Apple Watch (deferred to v1.2), the mark renders at 88, 100, 102, 108, and 117 px @2x — re-uses the same SVG.

Don'ts Do not place the mark on a light background. Do not invert the triangles. Do not add a glow, shadow, or stroke. Do not pair the wordmark with a tagline ("Built for the rack", "Lift smarter") — the app is the tagline.

Icon + label rule

Icons never stand alone. Every icon in the app appears next to a text label, a number, or both. The icon is decoration; the text carries the meaning.

HR142bpm
KCAL87kcal
RSSI−54dBm
When in doubt, skip the icon A label and a number is almost always clearer than a label, an icon, and a number. The app is an instrument, not a Material design.
